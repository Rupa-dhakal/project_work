Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This page explains how InkWell uses cookies and similar technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, making it more useful on your next visit.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
session Keeps you logged in and stores flash messages between page loads. Until browser close or logout Essential

Cookies We Do NOT Use

  • Advertising or marketing cookies
  • Analytics tracking cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party profiling cookies

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling the session cookie will prevent you from logging in. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, and block cookies from specific websites via the Settings → Privacy menu.
